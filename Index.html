The purpose of Treble is to allow users to rate and discover music from around the world and learn information about specific songs and artists. Users on Treble can navigate through different artists and genres to find and rate all kinds of music. Each song on the website will have links to listen on streaming platforms like YouTube, Spotify, and Soundcloud.

Our target audience is music listeners who want to understand how people perceive the music that they listen to and contribute their own inputs with ratings and reviews. Treble also allows users to discover music in the genres they are interested in exploring further. Treble can also display the highest rated music from each genre, along with a link to the reviews of the band or song.

All users will be able to contribute content by adding ratings to the songs they listen to and specific songs not already on the database. Users must register with their email and verify their accounts before they can participate in the community features we offer. Users can add songs if Treble does not contain the specific song they are looking for. Unregistered users are free to search through the expansive music database but cannot contribute anything to it.

A user will share one to one relationship with Userinfo. Userinfo will share one to one relationship with a review. Music will share one to one relationship with the review regarding ID. Unique ID will be assigned to Music name and review ID can access to the unique ID.
